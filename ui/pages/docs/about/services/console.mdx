---
title: Broker Console
---

Morio is a streaming data platform, and we leverage
[RedPanda](https://redpanda.com) to provide those streaming capabilities.

The RedPanda <a
href="https://poc-morio-node1.cert.europa.eu/console?cache_bust=1707487272954"
target="_BLANK">Console</a> is a web-based UI for managing Redpanda brokers.
It provides visibility into your streaming data and management of your topics,
consumer groups, and access control.
